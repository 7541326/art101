Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem Solving

Challenge

For this lab, we took an everyday task and a simple game, and broke both down into steps to practice pseudocoding.

Everyday Task Pseudocode

  1. Prep the natural nail bed
    1. Wash and dry hands
    2. Trim nails with a nail clipper to comportable length
    3. Using a rectangular nail buffer, buff/sand nail bed
    4. Apply nail dehydrator
    5. Apply nail primer
    6. Repeat with all finger nails
  2. Pick out a nail shape and sizes
  3. Prepare the gelx nail extension
    1. Slightly buff out the gelx nails with a nail drill or buffer on the bottom side of the nails
    2. Use acetone with a brush to brush out the nails for grip
    3. Apply nail primer to the bottom
    4. Repeat with all gelx nails
  4. Allow all nails to dry with primer
  5. Prepare a mini/accessible UV lamp for gluing
  6. Apply nail glue to the bottom side of the nail
  7. Place nails glue side down to natural nail bed
    1. Hover UV light over the nail after carefully placing with no air bubbles
      1. If air bubbles appear reapply with extra glue
      2. Clean off excess glue
    2. Repeat with all finger nails
  8. Put hands in large nail lamp one at a time, 1 minute each
  9. Reshape nails to desired shape and length
  10. Prep for nail design
    1. Buff the nails until all gelx tip are a foggy white
    2. Clean off dust with rubbing alcohol
    3. Apply base coat gel polish to all nails
    4. Then flash under the UV lamp for at most 30 seconds
  11. For white pearl nails
    1. Apply a milky white gel polish
    2. Flash nails under lamp for at max 30 seconds
    3. Use rubbing alcohol to remove sticky residue
    4. Using a eye shadow applicator take some pearl chrome powder and rub all over the nails
      1. Dust off excess powder
    5. Seal with top coat
  12. Clean off nails with rubbing alcohol
  13. Finish with cuticle oil
